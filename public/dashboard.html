Help Guide

Simple 3-Step Setup

Quickly calculate and predict your attendance status!

1 Step 1: Add Your Subjects

Click on Subjects in the menu. Type class names and press + to add them.

2 Step 2: Build Your Timetable

Click on Timetable. Tap period slots to assign subjects for each day of the week.

3 Step 3: Mark Daily Attendance

Click on Attendance. Tap subject cards to toggle status: Present (Green) or Absent (Red).

Detailed Feature Guide

Dashboard

Displays total attendance percentage, subject cards, and automatic predictors advising how many classes are required to maintain target percentages.

Subjects

Add or delete subject names. Changes update dynamically across your timetable and calendar.

Timetable

Build your weekly schedule. Click + to add period slots for any day of the week.

Attendance & Markers

Tap calendar dates to view or edit logs. Set a Start Marker to define when logging begins. Use bulk actions for quick marking.

How to Use

Simple Guide

Follow these 3 easy steps to track your attendance!

Step 1: Add Your Subjects

Click on Subjects in the menu. Type the name of your class and press the + button. Do this for all your classes!

Step 2: Make Your Timetable

Click on Timetable. You will see empty boxes like P1, P2. Just tap a box and pick which subject you have at that time. Do this for every day of the week.

Step 3: Mark Your Attendance

Click on Attendance. Look at today's list. Tap the subject to say if you were there:
• Green means you were Present.
• Red means you were Absent.

Detailed Menu Guide

📊 Dashboard

The Dashboard is your home page. It shows your Overall Attendance at the bottom. Each subject has a card showing your percentage.
• If you see a Predictor message, it tells you exactly how many classes you need to attend to stay above 75%.

📚 Subjects

List all your classes here.
• Click the Green + to add a subject.
• Type the name and it saves as you type.
• Use the Red X to delete a subject. Warning: This will also delete all attendance for that subject!

📅 Timetable

Set up your weekly schedule.
• Use the Day Buttons (Mon, Tue...) to switch days.
• Click the Green + to add a new period (like P1, P2).
• Tap a slot to pick a subject for that period.
• Use the small X on a slot to remove it.

✅ Attendance & Calendar Markers

The place where you record your daily presence.
• Start Marker: Click "Set Start Marker Here" to set the date when your attendance logs officially begin. You cannot edit attendance logs before this date. To change it, remove the old one first.
• Calendar: Tap any date to see what you had that day.
• Marking: Tap a subject card to change it: Present (Green) or Absent (Red).
• Bulk Actions: Use "Mark All Present" to save time if you attended all classes.
• Extra Class: Use this if you had a special class that wasn't in your normal timetable.
• Calendar Day Markers: Look for letters on the calendar dates:
- S (Blue): **Start Marker** – The start date of your attendance logs. Logs before this date are frozen and cannot be edited.
- E (Purple): **Extra Class** – Shows that you have recorded an extra class on that day.
- C (Grey/Red Dot): **Cancelled** – Indicates a scheduled class was cancelled on that day.
- P (Yellow): **Pending** – A scheduled period that hasn't been marked yet (needs action!).

👤 Profile

Manage your account.
• Name: Change how your name appears on the dashboard.
• User ID: This is to help search database logs quickly.
• Change Password: Click this button in the Profile card to change your password securely.

🔑 Password Recovery & OTP

Secure authentication and password reset:
• Forgot Password: Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page. Enter your email to receive a secure **6-digit OTP** via Gmail, then input the OTP and your new password to instantly regain access!
• Security: All security alerts, password changes, and OTPs are officially sent to your inbox from adcal.auth@gmail.com.
